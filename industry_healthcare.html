Results

Customer & Market Due Diligence of Outpatient Physical Therapy Provider

Developed clarity on the target’s competitive positioning, reputation among its referral base, and future growth opportunities; interviewed 170 medical professionals, analyzed government data on procedures/injuries, and built a bottom-up market size/growth model for the physical therapy market in current and expansion metropolitan areas

Exit Preparation/Positioning for Niche Surgical Training Products Supplier

Developed clear and credible narrative regarding market structure, competitive dynamics, and growth opportunities in information-poor market by: conducting ~50 interviews of medical device companies, universities, and training labs; profiling major competitors (business model, size, geographic scope, certifications, facilities, etc.); and triangulating market size

Rapid Backlog Reduction at Precision Medical Device Component Manufacturer

Within 3 months, improved on-time performance from 59% to 93% while shrinking lead time from 13-16 weeks to 10 weeks and halving backlog by implementing prioritized schedule and debottlenecking strategies, activating ERP planning and scheduling functionality, cleaning up system data/parameters, and revamping engineering process

Customer & Market and Operational Due Diligence of Private Pay Residential Mental Health Services Provider

Phase I focused on understanding the overall market and referral dynamics, the target’s overall competitive positioning, and the growth opportunity; Phase II addressed program-level operational performance, reputation/ competitive positioning, and recessionary risk

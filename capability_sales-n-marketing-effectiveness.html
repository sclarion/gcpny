Results

Salesforce Effectiveness at Direct Marketing Services Company

Developed a sales organization structure that included removing underperformers, reorganizing salesforce by industry, reallocating sales support staff, hiring “less expensive” salespeople, and implementing a new compensation structure to incentivize new business generation; identified key sales & marketing need for new digital and analytics solutions, with savings to be invested accordingly

Strategic Pricing Capabilities at Large Catalog Company

Put in place necessary fact base and analytical processes to enable proper pricing decisions in a market with distinct price thresholds; created pricing guidelines and robust pricing process, including pricing constraints/requirements, category price point matrix, time-based pricing, and KPIs; immediately used to reprice 1,100 items, with a margin increase of $1.3MM

Sales Function Redesign for Flow Control Products Distributor

Developed salesforce effectiveness plan to support the growth strategy: professionalized inside sales by redesigning the organization and SOPs to increase productivity, providing training on key product attributes, and creating processes for proactive customer management; developed a robust outside sales function, including specific requirements for regional offices and standardized sales processes/tools

Strategic Commission Structure at Staffing Company

Dissected the company’s cost and job performance to establish 20-30% EBITDA improvement opportunity from growing select high-margin niches and improving profitability of lower-margin clients; redesigned sales and recruiting commission structure to incent salesforce to pursue desirable business, and built a sophisticated tool to simulate commission scenarios

Results

Customer & Market Due Diligence of In-store Bakery Supplier

Established in-store bakery market dynamics; conducted consumer panel surveys to establish buying behavior and test potential concepts for driving conversion; interviewed 37 retail executives and surveyed 276 in-store bakery employees to establish their decision-making criteria and plans; and modeled a market size matrix to estimate the conversion opportunity

Operational Due Diligence of National Coffee Roaster and Distributor

Despite limited access to the target’s management, plants, and operations data, created a clear picture of the target’s cost structure in both operations and its DSD network; while the cost reduction opportunities needed to achieve our client’s financial target did exist, capturing those savings posed significant culture and business strategy challenges

Operational Transformation at Foodservice Juice Manufacturer

Turned around plant performance by implementing basic plant operating system (downtime, waste, and scheduling); $600K in run rate cost savings captured by the end of the 6-week engagement, with tools and processes in place to capture the rest; rolled out the standard operating procedures to the other 2 plants with additional savings of $2-2.5MM

Operations Diagnostic at National Seafood Company

Identified distribution cost savings of $1.5-2MM on a base of $7MM from: changing package size to reduce ice consumption; increasing fleet utilization by improving route planning; changing policies and upstream processes (e.g., order policies, production planning, inventory management, load layout); and changing driver compensation structure

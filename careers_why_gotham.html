Why Gotham?

A Private Equity consulting firm offering great diversity of work, unparalleled learning opportunity, and first-hand exposure to leaders of consulting, private equity, and industry

Gotham Consulting Partners specializes in working with private equity firms and their portfolio companies on their most impactful C-level challenges. Serving leading private equity firms throughout the investment lifecycle, Gotham consultants are exposed to a wide spectrum of potential and current portfolio companies, encompassing a variety of sizes, industries, and situations.

A Gotham career involves a diverse set of responsibilities, from detailed analysis of financials to advising corporate leaders on operations and business strategy. Gotham consultants are intelligent, ambitious, and personable individuals who embrace challenges and are willing and able to assume multiple responsibilities.

Recruited from the top engineering and business schools as well as from industry, our team is composed of superior and proactive problem solvers and implementers who are looking to make a difference. Working in a team environment (both with Gotham and with client personnel), our consultants are also entrepreneurial who are motivated by and value frequent exposure to senior-level management, substantive intellectual challenges, and clear ownership of the content and quality of the workstream.

GCPNY logo

Gotham Consulting Partners LLC specializes in rapidly creating significant value for private equity firms and privately-held companies. To deliver this value, we create proactive investment strategies, perform robust due diligence, craft pragmatic growth strategies, identify profitability-/growth-driving operations improvement opportunities, and rapidly execute changes that maximize financial benefits.

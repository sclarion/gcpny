Results

Customer & Market Due Diligence of Plumbing and Drain Cleaning Services Company

Validated defensibility of target’s residential business and established/confirmed growth opportunity in commercial business by conducting consumer panel and customer surveys, commercial customer surveys and interviews across 11 end-markets, and deep-dive analysis of several years of job-level data; detailed the competitive landscapes in each of the target’s territories

Operations Diagnostic and Roadmap at Engineering Services Firm

Provided a solid roadmap for capitalizing on levers to achieve 5-7% margin growth and position the company for a high-valuation exit in 2-3 years’ time; analyzed historical project and employee utilization performance; developed staffing leverage model; created business development model to improve efficiency while driving growth; and developed strategic pricing approach

Operational Due Diligence of Testing Services Provider

Identified and quantified facility consolidation and other synergies; determined cost additions and operations improvement opportunities; and established current asset and labor utilization by building a comprehensive and reliable cost/capacity fact base, conducting detailed tours of all the facilities, and benchmarking operational metrics across the 2 companies

Customer & Market Due Diligence of Specialty Engineering Consulting Firm

Defined complex niche-market dynamics, validated target’s competitive positioning, established criteria driving customer buying behavior, and provided clarity on growth opportunities moving forward by interviewing 60+ customers, profiling over 180 competitors, and conducting extensive research on market dynamics
